Project Overview

This applications purpose it to display photos of the previous cakes made by my client and allow new customers to contact my client

The intended users are anyone who is interested in purchasing a custom cake. This cake can be for a birthday, wedding, or anything in between.

This application will show photos, share contact information, and show prices for the different sizes of cakes.

Page Design

Homepage

This page is the first page that customers will view. It will show a photo and give clickable buttons/ links to the photo album page, price page, and the about page

About Page

This page will be filled with the story of how Cake Creations by Alex was sdtarted, Alex's credentials in cake baking and decorating, and also link to the contact page with a button

Price Page

This page will used as a guide to customers on what prices are for different sized cakes, these options will give the cake dimensions and general price for standard decorating. This page will have a button link to the contact page

Contact Page

This page will show all contact information for Alex. It will also give a brief summary on how to contact and when to contact Alex for a cake as the process is different when ordering a wedding cake over a standard birthday or general style cake

Photo Album

This page will have a slideshow of photos of previous cakes made by Alex and allow the user to select and access a larger version of the image. The photos will be shown originally in a grid style but the user can click on any photo and a larger version of that photo will pop up with an exit button to leave that specific photo
